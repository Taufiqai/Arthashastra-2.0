Fiscal — Ministry of Finance
Full implementation adds ≈ Tk 1,06,000 crore of permanent recurrent spending; the FY27 plan funds roughly Tk 33,000 crore (≈31%).
FY26 collection is tracking ≈ Tk 4.0 lac crore. The FY27 budget target of Tk 6.04 lac crore implies ~51% growth — recent years have delivered 10–15%.
Financing mix — incremental deficit
WMA/overdraft and devolvement: creates reserve money one-for-one and works against the policy rate.
Lower monetary risk, but hardens after LDC graduation (Nov 2026). The remainder is borrowed from scheduled banks and NSS — the crowding-out channel.
Monetary — Bangladesh Bank
When NSS yields sit above the corridor, savings migrate out of banks and BB cannot anchor borrowing costs.
